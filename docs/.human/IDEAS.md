# Ideas

## Design Ideas (concrete)

- Remove the side panels and make them screw-in for access to speaker and electronics
- Figure out what bolts/fasteners I will use for the speaker drivers
- Decide on bolts/assembly for the side panel. Threaded inserts maybe?
- Add gasket grooves anywhere there are faces being joined
- Adapt perfectly to car interior geometry so that the speaker is snug and looks (somewhat) OEM

## Design Ideas (speculative)

- Add Keystone binding posts
- Make this an adaptable speaker that can be unplugged from the car and used inside
- Maybe add one of those holes that subwoofers are supposed to have? I think you need to do some calculations to figure out the hole diameter and placement and such
- Add some sort of button, switch, or knobs that remotely connect to the AMP and adjust settings (like turning on bass boost or something)
